Our Destinations

The Adriatic &
beyond — on your
terms.

We operate across the Adriatic and Balkans, selecting the right destination for each club's specific needs — from Croatia and Montenegro to Bosnia and beyond. Every location is chosen for climate, infrastructure and value.

Split & Dalmatia, Croatia Mediterranean climate, excellent connectivity and premium training infrastructure on the Adriatic coast.
Montenegro Budva Riviera and Bay of Kotor — competitive rates, Adriatic quality, ideal for squads seeking lower-profile preparation.
North Croatia & Istria Kvarner Gulf and Istrian peninsula — cooler pre-season temperatures, excellent facilities, premium hotels.
Fully Customisable No fixed packages. Every destination, facility and itinerary is sourced and confirmed around your squad's exact requirements.
